Welcome to Akshara Kids

( A Unit of Atharv kids Pvt.Ltd ) AKSHARA KIDS provides a safe and happy learning environment that gives utmost joy and a sense of home to the child. We lay an interactive platform to provide the children with a well-rounded curriculum to explore and learn. Having recognised the importance of pre-school education and the redundancy of rote learning in todays scenario, we have developed our curriculum to create thinkers who can analyze and apply their knowledge to real-life, problem-solving situations.

Physical Development

Physical Development relates to the growth and skill development of the body, including the brain muscles, and senses. We provide suitable activities according to the age and Group.

Language Development

Language development is essential for general milestones that act as an indicator in child's ability to communicate and develop their skills. Language skills are in a way that will express their thoughts, feeling and understands the language. Our Kinder Garden syllabus are based on creative teaching and individualized instruction with LSRW skills.

Cognitive Development

Cognitive Development is the way a child learns to think, reason, and to use language which are vital to the child's overall growth and development. We enhance this through exploration and play.
